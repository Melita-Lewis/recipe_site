Chocolate Cake

"Here is a fast and easy version of classic Chocolate cake Receipe. Enjoy!"

DifficultyPrepartionCookingTotal Time
Easy10 mins25 mins35 mins

Ingredients

Black Chocolate: 200g

Sugar: 150g

Butter: 200g

Eggs: 4

Flour: 80g

Instructions

1

Melt the Chocolate and Butter together

2

Mix Sugar , Eggs and the Flour

3

Mix everyting to get a smooth dough

4

Bake in oven
